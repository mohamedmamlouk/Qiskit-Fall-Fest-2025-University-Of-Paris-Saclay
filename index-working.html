About the Fall Fest

The Qiskit Fall Fest is a global series of quantum computing events that brings together students, researchers, and industry professionals to explore a diverse range of quantum-focused activities. These include hackathons, coding competitions, technical challenges, workshops, and social gatherings. Each edition is co-organized with selected university students and volunteer hosts worldwide, who help design and run the local events as part of the international program. This year's edition is particularly special, as it celebrates the centenary of quantum mechanics.
