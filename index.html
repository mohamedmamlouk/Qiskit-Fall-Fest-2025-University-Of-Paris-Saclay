About the Fall Fest

Join us from November 17–21, 2025, at Université Paris-Saclay for an immersive week in quantum computing. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, this event offers opportunities to learn, collaborate, and innovate. Hosted at a university renowned for its quantum excellence — home to Nobel laureates Alain Aspect (2022, quantum entanglement) and Michel Devoret (2025, superconducting qubits) — this edition celebrates 100 years of quantum mechanics and the pioneers driving its future. The program features hands-on workshops, quantum programming, hackathons, industry meetups, lab visits, and panel discussions, along with career guidance and networking sessions connecting academia and industry. Registration is free and open to all backgrounds — with 30 on-site places and unlimited online participation. Come be part of the global Qiskit community and help shape the future of quantum computing.
