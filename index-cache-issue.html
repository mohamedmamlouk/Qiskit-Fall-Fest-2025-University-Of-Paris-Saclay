About the Fall Fest

The Qiskit Fall Fest is a collection of quantum computing events around the world. This year's theme celebrates the 100th anniversary of quantum mechanics with cutting-edge workshops and collaborative innovation.

Join us at the University of Paris-Saclay for an intensive 4-day journey into the quantum realm, featuring hands-on workshops, expert talks, and a competitive hackathon.
